ANSUNG EDUCATION AND DEVELOPMEN IN RURAL GHANA
(AEDIRG)
“ANSUNG” MEANS TO SUPPORT / TO HELP

PREAMBLE:

AEDIRG is a Non-Governmental and Nonprofit Making Organization based in northern Ghana with ultimate aim of: Improving the level of education in rural Ghana, encouraged international and local volunteerism, Child sponsorship through foster parenting, Environmental protection through tree planting, Water and sanitation, youth sensitization in rural Ghana.

VISSION

In Ghana most especially the deprived communities in the Northern Region and its catchments areas are people live and work without being able to improve their standard of living. Most of the people living in these areas are peasant farmers and petty traders with minimal or no education at all, therefore cannot secure any significant well paying job. In this same Region most people can not secure good drinking water also parents do not see the need to send their children to school. Falling of trees indiscriminately without replacing is a key problem to the nation, youth sensitization in rural Ghana on substance use and volunteerism has not left out, this state of affairs has been partly due to financial problems and also ignorant, as a result “AEDIRG” is taking it as part of its responsibility to support these projects.

KEY OBJECTIVES:

  1. Improve the level of education in rural Ghana
  2. Volunteering in Northern Ghana
  3. Child sponsorship through foster parenting.
  4. Sensitization of the youth in rural Ghana on substance use
  5. Improve upon quality of water and sanitation

EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
(Mission)

  • AEDIRG as part of its responsibility: will Improve the level of education in rural areas this is done through the organization of seminars and workshops for teachers in rural parts of the northern region, also organized inter school quizzes and debate completions as well support less privilege children with books and other learning materials that may be necessary,
  • Volunteering in Northern Ghana, AEDIRG is an organization dedicated to providing year-round programs for local and international volunteers to interact in development process by participating in projects which support to enrich the lives of deprived children and women in rural communities. The organization aims to find volunteer placements and to provide support for individual or group volunteers in Northern Ghana. We aim at.
    Promoting volunteerism and voluntary efforts in social and national development. Encouraging international and local volunteers towards volunteerism to contribute to community development and nation building To fight poverty and strengthen the capacity of disadvantaged communities; support initiative for peace, promotes human rights and equity.
  • Child sponsorship through foster parenting will in one way or the other help some children in these areas who truly can not be in school as a result of poor financial bar ground and will on the same vein support the brelent but needy children,
  • Environmental protection through tree planting, it is clear that in all parts of the country there exist an indiscriminate falling of trees without replacing hence “AEDIRG” takes it as a responsibility to embark upon massive tree planting in other to reduced the green house effect as the saying goes ” as the last tree dies, the last man dies”
  • No one will dispute the fact that water takes 75% percent of the total earth surface; and yet people live in this noble country who cannot get good drinking water hence “AEDIRG” will take it as part of its responsibility to ensure that good drinking water reaches these people; no matter where they might be living.
  • Youth sensitization in the rural areas on substance use: this is done through the organization of seminars and workshops to enlighten the youth in these areas as to positive attitude that they should adapt in other to embraced development in their respective areas.
